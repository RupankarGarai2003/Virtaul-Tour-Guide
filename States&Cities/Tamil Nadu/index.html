Chennai

Marina Beach

India's longest urban beach with scenic views.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

Ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Fort St. George

Historic fort and museum showcasing colonial history.

San Thome Basilica

Roman Catholic basilica built over the tomb of Saint Thomas.

Mahabalipuram

Shore Temple

UNESCO World Heritage Site, ancient rock-cut temples by the sea.

Pancha Rathas (Five Rathas)

Monolithic rock-cut temples in the shape of chariots.

Arjuna's Penance

Giant open-air bas-relief depicting scenes from the Mahabharata.

Kanchipuram

Kanchi Kailasanathar Temple

Oldest temple in Kanchipuram, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Ekambareswarar Temple

Ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, with a thousand-pillar hall.

Madurai

Meenakshi Amman Temple

Iconic Hindu temple with towering gopurams (gateway towers).

Thirumalai Nayakkar Mahal

Grand palace built in Indo-Saracenic style.

Gandhi Memorial Museum

Museum showcasing Mahatma Gandhi's life and teachings.

Thanjavur

Brihadeeswarar Temple

UNESCO World Heritage Site, ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Thanjavur Palace

Royal palace complex with art gallery and Saraswathi Mahal Library.

Coimbatore

Marudamalai Murugan Temple

Hilltop temple dedicated to Lord Murugan.

Dhyanalinga

Meditation center and temple dedicated to spirituality.

Ooty

Ooty Botanical Gardens

Lush gardens with a variety of exotic plants and flowers.

Ooty Lake

Artificial lake offering boating and scenic views.

Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal Lake

Man-made lake surrounded by hills and lush greenery.

Bryant Park

Botanical garden with a wide variety of plants and flowers.

Rameswaram

Ramanathaswamy Temple

Famous Hindu temple with a sacred water tank (teertham).

Dhanushkodi

Ghost town and pilgrimage site near the tip of the Indian peninsula.

Tiruchirappalli

Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple

Largest functioning Hindu temple complex in the world.

Rockfort Temple

Ancient fort complex with temples and panoramic views.

Kanyakumari

Kanyakumari

Southernmost tip of mainland India, known for Vivekananda Rock Memorial and Thiruvalluvar Statue.

Chettinad

Chettinad

Region known for its unique Chettinad cuisine, mansions, and cultural heritage.

Festivals and Cultural Events

Pongal

Harvest festival celebrated with fervor across Tamil Nadu.

Natyanjali Dance Festival

Dance festival held annually at Chidambaram Nataraja Temple.

Adventure and Nature Activities

Trekking

Opportunities in the Western Ghats and Nilgiri Hills.

Wildlife Sanctuaries

Explore diverse flora and fauna in sanctuaries like Mudumalai and Indira Gandhi Wildlife Sanctuary.
