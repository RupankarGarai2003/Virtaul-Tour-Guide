Agra

UNESCO World Heritage Site, Mughal city with palaces and Jama Masjid

Mughal-era mausoleum known as the "Baby Taj"

Varanasi

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Holiest temple dedicated to Lord Shiva

Dashashwamedh Ghat

Famous ghat for Ganga Aarti (ritual offering to River Ganges)

Sarnath

Buddhist pilgrimage site with stupas, monasteries, and Ashoka Pillar

Adventure sport offering aerial views of Varanasi and River Ganges

Lucknow

Bara Imambara

Mughal-era complex with a labyrinth, mosque, and Bhul Bhulaiya

Chota Imambara

Shia Muslim congregation hall and mausoleum

Rumi Darwaza

Historic gate known as the "Turkish Gate"

Allahabad (Prayagraj)

Triveni Sangam

Confluence of three rivers (Ganges, Yamuna, and mythical Saraswati)

Allahabad Fort

Fortification built by Emperor Akbar, overlooking the confluence

Historic pillar erected by Emperor Ashoka
