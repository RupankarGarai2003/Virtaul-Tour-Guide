Amritsar

Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib)

Holiest Gurdwara of Sikhism, known for its golden architecture and serene lake (Sarovar).

Jallianwala Bagh

Memorial garden commemorating the Jallianwala Bagh massacre of 1919.

Wagah Border

Border between India and Pakistan, famous for the Beating Retreat ceremony.

Durgiana Temple

Hindu temple resembling the Golden Temple, dedicated to Goddess Durga.

Maharaja Ranjit Singh Museum

Museum showcasing artifacts related to Maharaja Ranjit Singh and Sikh history.

Chandigarh

Rock Garden

Sculpture garden made from industrial and home waste.

Sukhna Lake

Artificial lake offering boating and scenic views.

Rose Garden (Zakir Hussain Rose Garden)

Largest rose garden in Asia, with thousands of rose varieties.

Patiala

Qila Mubarak Complex

Fort and palace complex showcasing Sikh architecture and artifacts.

Sheesh Mahal

Palace with mirrored walls and ceilings.

Baradari Gardens

Mughal-style gardens with pavilions and fountains.

Ludhiana

Nehru Rose Garden

Rose garden with landscaped lawns and musical fountain.

Punjab Agricultural University Museum

Museum showcasing agricultural innovations and rural life.

Jalandhar

Devi Talab Mandir

Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Durga.

Wonderland Theme Park

Amusement park with water rides and entertainment.

Anandpur Sahib

Anandpur Sahib

Sikh pilgrimage site, known for the Anandpur Sahib Gurudwara and historic significance.

Virasat-e-Khalsa

Museum showcasing Sikh history and culture.

Bathinda

Qila Mubarak

Historic fort with ancient artifacts and architecture.

Chetak Park

Park with amusement rides and recreational facilities.

Other Attractions

Fatehgarh Sahib

Sikh pilgrimage site, known for historical gurudwaras.

Ropar (Rupnagar)

Archaeological site with ancient Indus Valley Civilization remains at Harappa.

Festivals and Cultural Events

Baisakhi

Harvest festival celebrated with fervor in Punjab, especially at Anandpur Sahib.

Lohri

Winter festival celebrated with bonfires, music, and dance.

Adventure and Nature Activities

Punjab Rural Heritage Museum (Pind Sangraur)

Museum showcasing rural life and artifacts.

Punjab Wildlife Sanctuaries

Wildlife sanctuaries offering birdwatching and nature walks.
