Top Famous Tourist Places in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a beautiful coastal city known for its stunning beaches, rich history, and scenic hills.

Ramakrishna Beach

Ramakrishna Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Visakhapatnam, known for its picturesque views and vibrant atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy beach activities, local food stalls, and the iconic Dolphin's Nose lighthouse nearby.

Kailasagiri

Kailasagiri is a hilltop park offering panoramic views of the city and the Bay of Bengal. The park features lush greenery, a giant statue of Lord Shiva, and numerous recreational activities, making it a popular tourist destination.

Submarine Museum

The Submarine Museum, located on the beach, is dedicated to the INS Kurusura, a decommissioned submarine. It offers visitors a glimpse into life aboard a submarine and showcases naval history through various exhibits.

Yarada Beach

Yarada Beach is a serene and less crowded beach known for its scenic beauty and tranquility. Surrounded by lush hills, it is an ideal spot for relaxation and enjoying stunning sunsets.

Borra Caves

The Borra Caves are limestone caves known for their stunning stalactite and stalagmite formations. Located about 90 kilometers from Visakhapatnam, they are a popular destination for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

VUDA Park

VUDA Park is a well-maintained park located along the beach, offering a peaceful environment with beautiful landscapes, walking trails, and recreational facilities. It is perfect for family outings and evening strolls.
