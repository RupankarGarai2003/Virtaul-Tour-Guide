Gangtok

Rumtek Monastery

Buddhist monastery, seat of the Karmapa Lama.

Enchey Monastery

Ancient monastery with scenic views of Gangtok.

Namgyal Institute of Tibetology

Museum and research center on Tibetan culture and Buddhism.

Tashi View Point

Viewpoint offering panoramic views of Kanchenjunga and the Himalayas.

MG Marg

Pedestrian street lined with shops, cafes, and restaurants.

Tsomgo Lake

Tsomgo Lake

Glacial lake at an altitude of 12,310 feet, known for its changing colors.

Baba Mandir

Shrine dedicated to Indian Army soldier Baba Harbhajan Singh.

Nathu La Pass

Nathu La Pass

Indo-China border crossing at an altitude of 14,140 feet.

North Sikkim

Gurudongmar Lake

Sacred lake at an altitude of 17,800 feet, surrounded by snow-capped mountains.

Yumthang Valley

Valley known as the "Valley of Flowers" with hot springs.

Pelling

Pemayangtse Monastery

Ancient monastery with religious artworks and panoramic views.

Rabdentse Ruins

Ruins of the ancient capital of Sikkim, offering scenic views.

Yuksom

Dubdi Monastery

Oldest monastery in Sikkim, known for its peaceful surroundings.

Khangchendzonga National Park

UNESCO World Heritage Site, home to diverse flora and fauna.

Ravangla

Ralong Monastery

Buddhist monastery with scenic views of mountains and tea gardens.

Buddha Park (Tathagata Tsal)

Park with a giant statue of Buddha overlooking the town.

Namchi

Samdruptse Monastery

Monastery with a giant statue of Guru Padmasambhava (Guru Rinpoche).

Char Dham

Pilgrimage site with replicas of four sacred pilgrimage centers of India.

Other Attractions

Zuluk

Hilltop village offering views of the Himalayas and winding roads (Old Silk Route).

Teesta River Rafting

Adventure activity in the Teesta River's rapids.

Festivals and Cultural Events

Losar

Tibetan New Year celebrated with rituals, dance, and music.

Saga Dawa

Buddhist festival commemorating the birth, enlightenment, and death of Buddha.

Adventure and Nature Activities

Trekking and Hiking

Trails like Goecha La Trek, Dzongri Trek, and Green Lake Trek.

Birdwatching

Opportunities to spot Himalayan birds in various sanctuaries and parks.
