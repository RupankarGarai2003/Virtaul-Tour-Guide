Tourist Places in Chandigarh

  • Cities and Places

Mohali

  • Sukhna Lake: A beautiful artificial lake ideal for boating and leisure activities, surrounded by scenic views.
  • Mohali Cricket Stadium: A prominent cricket venue that hosts national and international matches, known for its vibrant atmosphere.
  • Punjab Cricket Association (PCA) Stadium: A state-of-the-art stadium that has hosted numerous cricket tournaments and events.
  • Rock Garden: A unique garden made from industrial and urban waste, showcasing creative sculptures and artwork.
  • Phase 3B2 Park: A popular recreational area with walking trails, playgrounds, and green spaces for families.
  • Mohali Gurudwara: A serene place of worship, known for its beautiful architecture and peaceful atmosphere.

New Chandigarh

  • Punjab Engineering College (PEC): A prestigious engineering institute known for its campus and academic excellence.
  • Sector 30 Park: A well-maintained park perfect for jogging, picnics, and enjoying nature.
  • International Technology Park: A hub for tech companies, fostering innovation and entrepreneurship.
  • Green Park: A large green space ideal for relaxation, outdoor activities, and community events.
  • Gurudwara Sahib: A serene place of worship, known for its peaceful ambiance and community services.
  • Shopping Malls: New Chandigarh features various shopping malls offering retail, dining, and entertainment options.

Patiala

  • Qila Mubarak: A historic fort complex showcasing exquisite Mughal architecture and rich history.
  • Sheesh Mahal: A stunning palace known for its beautiful mirror work and picturesque gardens.
  • Patiala Heritage Museum: A museum featuring artifacts that highlight the cultural and historical significance of Patiala.
  • Baradari Garden: A beautiful garden ideal for relaxation, featuring fountains and lush greenery.
  • Ali Club: A historic club offering various recreational activities and a glimpse into the royal heritage.
  • Gurdwara Dukh Niwaran Sahib: A significant religious site attracting many visitors and devotees.

Zirakpur

  • Elante Mall: One of the largest shopping malls in the region, offering a variety of brands, dining options, and entertainment.
  • Chhatbir Zoo: A popular zoo that houses various species of animals, perfect for family outings.
  • Sukhna Lake: A serene lake known for its picturesque views and recreational activities like boating.
  • Mahendra Park: A well-maintained park ideal for morning walks, jogging, and family gatherings.
  • Punjab Engineering College: A renowned engineering institution known for its academic excellence and campus.
  • Fun City: An amusement park with various rides and attractions for kids and adults alike.

Panchkula

  • Pinjore Gardens: A historic garden known for its beautiful landscaping, fountains, and Mughal architecture.
  • Mansa Devi Temple: A revered temple situated on the hills, offering stunning views and spiritual significance.
  • Panchkula Golf Course: A scenic golf course that attracts both amateurs and professionals for a day of leisure.
  • Shivalik Park: A large park with walking paths, playgrounds, and open spaces for recreation.
  • ISkcon Temple: A serene temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, offering spiritual gatherings and events.
  • Cactus Garden: The largest collection of cacti in India, located in nearby Panchkula, perfect for nature lovers.
