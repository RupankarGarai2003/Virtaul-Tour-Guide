Top Famous Tourist Places in Kozhikode, Kerala

Kozhikode, known for its historical significance and beautiful landscapes, offers a mix of cultural experiences and scenic beauty.

Kozhikode Beach

Kozhikode Beach is a popular destination for both locals and tourists, offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea, a peaceful atmosphere, and beautiful sunsets. The beach is also known for its historic lighthouse and the nearby fishing harbor.

Mananchira Square

Mananchira Square is a beautifully landscaped park in the heart of Kozhikode. It features a large pond, lush greenery, and various facilities for recreation. The square is a popular spot for locals to relax and enjoy cultural events.

Kappad Beach

Kappad Beach is famous for its historical significance, as it was the landing site of Vasco da Gama in 1498. This serene beach is lined with coconut palms and offers a tranquil environment, perfect for relaxation and enjoying nature.

Kozhikode Durbar Hall

The Kozhikode Durbar Hall is a historical building that showcases the architectural brilliance of the bygone era. It hosts various cultural events and exhibitions, reflecting the rich heritage of the region.

Calicut Lighthouse

The Calicut Lighthouse is a historic lighthouse located on the coastline of Kozhikode. It offers panoramic views of the sea and the surrounding area, making it a popular spot for photography and sightseeing.

Thusharagiri Waterfalls

Thusharagiri Waterfalls is a stunning waterfall nestled in the Western Ghats, surrounded by lush greenery. It is a popular trekking destination and offers breathtaking views, making it a great spot for nature lovers.
