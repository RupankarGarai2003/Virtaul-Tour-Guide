Agartala

Ujjayanta Palace: Former royal palace now housing a museum and gardens.

Neermahal: Lake palace built in the middle of Rudrasagar Lake.

Tripura Government Museum: Museum showcasing tribal culture, sculptures, and artifacts.

Unakoti

Unakoti: Archaeological site with rock-cut sculptures and ancient temples.

Pilak

Pilak: Archaeological site with Buddhist and Hindu sculptures and ruins.

Jampui Hills

Jampui Hills: Hill station known for its pleasant climate and orange orchards.

Vanghmun Village: Scenic village offering panoramic views of the hills.

Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary

Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary: Wildlife sanctuary with diverse flora, fauna, and a zoo.

Udaipur

Tripura Sundari Temple: Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Tripura Sundari.

Dumboor Lake

Dumboor Lake: Natural lake known for its scenic beauty and water sports.

Chabimura

Chabimura (Devtamura): Cliffs with rock carvings and scenic views of Gomati River.

Kalapania Nature Park

Kalapania Nature Park: Nature park with wildlife, trekking trails, and panoramic views.

Festivals and Cultural Events

Garia Puja: Traditional tribal festival celebrating worship of Lord Garia.

Kharchi Puja: Hindu festival dedicated to Tripura's royal deities.

Adventure and Nature Activities

Trekking and Hiking: Opportunities in the hills and forests of Tripura.

Boating and Water Sports: Activities at Dumboor Lake and other water bodies.
