French Quarter (Ville Blanche)

Promenade Beach

Popular beachfront promenade with a statue of Mahatma Gandhi.

French War Memorial

Memorial honoring French soldiers who died in World War I.

Rue de la Marine and Rue Dumas

Colonial-era streets with French architecture, cafes, and boutiques.

Aurobindo Ashram

Spiritual center founded by Sri Aurobindo and The Mother (Mirra Alfassa).

Auroville

Matrimandir

Golden spherical temple symbolizing unity and meditation.

Auroville Visitor's Centre

Information center about Auroville's philosophy and community.

Temples and Churches

Sri Manakula Vinayagar Temple

Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha.

Basilica of the Sacred Heart of Jesus

Catholic church with Gothic architecture and stained glass windows.

Museums and Art Galleries

Pondicherry Museum

Museum showcasing archaeological findings and French colonial artifacts.

Aurodhan Art Gallery

Gallery featuring contemporary Indian art and exhibitions.

Beaches

Paradise Beach

Remote beach accessible by boat, known for its crystal-clear waters.

Auroville Beach

Quiet beach popular for swimming and relaxation.

Other Attractions

Botanical Garden

Garden with exotic plants, an aquarium, and a musical fountain.

Chunnambar Boat House

Backwater boating and picnic spot near Paradise Beach.

Festivals and Events

Puducherry International Film Festival

Annual film festival showcasing international and Indian cinema.

Bastille Day

French National Day celebrated with cultural events in Puducherry.

Cuisine

French Bakeries and Cafes

Enjoy French pastries, crepes, and coffee in quaint cafes.
