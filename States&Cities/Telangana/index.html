Hyderabad

Charminar

Iconic monument and mosque with four minarets.

Golconda Fort

Historic fort known for its acoustics and light & sound show.

Salar Jung Museum

Museum with a vast collection of art, artifacts, and antiques.

Ramoji Film City

Largest film studio complex in the world, offering tours and entertainment.

Qutub Shahi Tombs

Tombs of the Qutub Shahi dynasty rulers, known for their architectural splendor.

Warangal

Warangal Fort

Ancient fort ruins with temples and stone gateways.

Thousand Pillar Temple

Kakatiya era temple known for its architecture and carved pillars.

Bhadrakali Temple

Temple dedicated to Goddess Bhadrakali.

Nagarjuna Sagar

Nagarjuna Sagar Dam

Largest masonry dam in the world, offering scenic views and boat rides.

Ethipothala Waterfalls

Scenic waterfall near Nagarjuna Sagar Dam.

Nizamabad

Nizamabad Fort

Historic fort with architectural remains.

Nizamabad Archaeological Museum

Museum showcasing artifacts from the region's history.

Karimnagar

Elgandal Fort (Golconda of Karimnagar)

Fort with historical significance and panoramic views.

Lower Manair Dam

Dam with a park and boating facilities.

Khammam

Khammam Fort

Hilltop fort with ancient ruins and views of the city.

Kinnerasani Wildlife Sanctuary

Wildlife sanctuary with diverse flora and fauna.

Adilabad

Kuntala Waterfalls

Highest waterfall in Telangana, surrounded by lush greenery.

Pochera Waterfalls

Scenic waterfall with natural pools for swimming.

Mahabubnagar

Pillalamarri

Banyan tree with a canopy covering an area of 3 acres.

Jurala Dam

Scenic dam on the Krishna River, offering boating and fishing.

Other Attractions

Medak Church

One of the largest churches in India, known for its stunning architecture.

Warangal Kakatiya Ruins

Ruins of the Kakatiya dynasty with historical significance.

Bathukamma

Deccan Festival

Cultural festival showcasing Telangana's music, dance, and cuisine.

Trekking and Hiking

Opportunities in various hills and nature reserves.

Wildlife Sanctuaries

Explore diverse wildlife in sanctuaries like Kawal Wildlife Sanctuary.
