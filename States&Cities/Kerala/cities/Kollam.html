Top Famous Tourist Places in Kollam, Kerala

Kollam, located along the picturesque Kerala backwaters, is known for its scenic beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture. Here are some must-visit places in Kollam.

Ashtamudi Lake

Ashtamudi Lake is the second largest lake in Kerala and a key feature of Kollam's backwaters. Known for its stunning beauty and rich biodiversity, it offers houseboat rides and scenic views, making it a popular destination for tourists.

Kollam Beach

Kollam Beach is a serene destination known for its long stretches of golden sand and tranquil atmosphere. It's an ideal spot for relaxation and offers breathtaking views of the sunset.

Jatayu Earth's Center

Jatayu Earth's Center is a unique adventure park that features the world's largest bird sculpture. The park offers various activities, including rock climbing, zip-lining, and an impressive 6D theater.

Thangassery Lighthouse

The Thangassery Lighthouse, built in 1902, is a historical landmark that offers panoramic views of the Arabian Sea. Visitors can climb to the top for breathtaking views of the coastline.

Palaruvi Waterfall

Palaruvi Waterfall, meaning 'stream of milk', is a stunning waterfall located amidst lush greenery. It is a perfect spot for nature lovers and those looking for a serene escape.

Azhimalathara Beach

Azhimalathara Beach is a serene and less-crowded beach, perfect for a peaceful day by the sea. The golden sands and gentle waves provide a perfect backdrop for relaxation.
