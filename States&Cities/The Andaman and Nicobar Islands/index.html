Port Blair

Historic colonial prison used by the British to exile political prisoners.

Historic ruins of British administrative buildings and a small museum.

Museum showcasing the indigenous tribes and culture of the islands.

Corbyn's Cove Beach

Scenic beach with water sports and sunset views.

Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep)

Radhanagar Beach

Pristine beach known for its white sand and turquoise waters.

Elephant Beach

Beach known for snorkeling and coral reefs.

Kalapathar Beach

Scenic beach with black rocks and clear waters.

Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep)

Laxmanpur Beach

Beach known for sunset views and natural rock formations.

Bharatpur Beach

Beach with coral reefs and opportunities for snorkeling and glass-bottom boat rides.

Baratang Island

Mud Volcano

Natural phenomenon where mud bubbles up due to volcanic activity.

Limestone Caves

Caves formed by limestone deposits over centuries.

North Andaman Island

Diglipur

Town known for its beaches, turtle nesting sites, and Saddle Peak (highest point in the islands).

Ross and Smith Islands

Twin islands connected by a natural sandbar, ideal for snorkeling and swimming.

Little Andaman Island

Butler Bay Beach

Remote beach known for surfing and elephant safaris.

Nicobar Islands (Restricted Area)

Car Nicobar

Island with scenic beaches and indigenous Nicobarese culture.

Great Nicobar

Largest island with rainforests, endemic wildlife, and the Indira Point (southernmost point of India).

Trekking and Hiking

Opportunities in the rainforests and hills of the islands.
