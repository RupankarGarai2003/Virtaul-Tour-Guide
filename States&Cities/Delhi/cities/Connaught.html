Top Famous Tourist Places in Connaught Place (CP), Delhi

Connaught Place (CP), one of the most iconic landmarks in Delhi, is known for its vibrant atmosphere, historic significance, and architectural beauty.

Jantar Mantar

Jantar Mantar is an ancient astronomical observatory built by Maharaja Jai Singh II in 1724. It consists of several structures designed for astronomical calculations and observations. This historical site in the heart of Connaught Place is a testament to India's scientific advancements in the past.

Palika Bazaar

Palika Bazaar is an underground market located in Connaught Place. Known for its electronic goods, clothing, and accessories, it attracts bargain hunters from all over the city. The market is a shopper's paradise and a great place to experience Delhi's local shopping culture.

Gurudwara Bangla Sahib

Gurudwara Bangla Sahib is one of the most famous Sikh temples in Delhi. Located near Connaught Place, it is known for its peaceful ambiance and the large pond, called Sarovar, where devotees take a holy dip. The Gurudwara offers langar (free meals) to everyone, making it a symbol of service and compassion.

Janpath Market

Janpath Market is a popular shopping destination near Connaught Place. It offers a wide variety of products, including clothes, jewelry, handicrafts, and souvenirs. The market is known for its affordable prices and is a must-visit for anyone looking for a vibrant shopping experience.

Agrasen ki Baoli

Agrasen ki Baoli is an ancient stepwell located near Connaught Place. It is a protected monument under the Archaeological Survey of India and is known for its impressive architecture and historical significance. The stepwell is a peaceful escape from the bustling city and a popular spot for photography.

Central Park

Central Park, located at the heart of Connaught Place, is a beautiful green space offering a peaceful retreat amidst the city's chaos. The park features lush lawns, walking paths, and a large Indian flag, making it a popular spot for picnics and evening strolls.
