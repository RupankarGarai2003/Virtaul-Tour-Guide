Dehradun

Robber's Cave (Guchhupani)

Natural cave formation with a stream inside.

Sahastradhara

"Thousand fold spring" with sulfur water springs and caves.

Mussoorie

Kempty Falls

Scenic waterfall with natural pools for swimming.

Gun Hill

Second highest peak of Mussoorie offering panoramic views.

Mall Road

Popular shopping and dining street with colonial architecture.

Rishikesh

Lakshman Jhula and Ram Jhula

Suspension bridges over the Ganges River.

Triveni Ghat

Sacred ghat for Ganga Aarti and holy dips.

Neelkanth Mahadev Temple

Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Haridwar

Har Ki Pauri

Sacred ghat with evening Ganga Aarti.

Chandi Devi Temple and Mansa Devi Temple

Hilltop temples dedicated to Goddess Chandi and Mansa Devi.

Nainital

Naini Lake

Scenic lake surrounded by hills and boat rides.

Naina Devi Temple

Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Naina Devi.

Snow View Point

Viewpoint offering panoramic views of the Himalayas.

Jim Corbett National Park

Jim Corbett National Park

Oldest national park in India, known for Bengal tigers and wildlife safaris.

Dhikala Zone

Wildlife zone offering jeep safaris and elephant rides.

Kedarnath and Badrinath

Kedarnath Temple

Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, one of the Char Dhams.

Valley of Flowers National Park

Valley of Flowers National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its alpine flowers and meadows.

Auli

Auli is a ski resort offering panoramic views of the Himalayas, popular among skiing enthusiasts.

Gurso Bugyal is a meadow near Auli known for trekking and views of Nanda Devi peak.

Other Attractions

Ranikhet

Ranikhet is a hill station offering views of the Himalayas and famous for Jhula Devi Temple.

Almora

Almora is a hill town known for its cultural heritage and the Nanda Devi Temple.

Festivals and Cultural Events

Kumbh Mela in Haridwar

Kumbh Mela is a massive religious gathering held every 12 years in Haridwar.

Ganga Dussehra in Haridwar

Ganga Dussehra is a festival celebrating the descent of River Ganga to Earth, observed in Haridwar.

Adventure and Nature Activities

Trekking and Hiking

Uttarakhand offers numerous trekking routes including Roopkund Trek and Valley of Flowers Trek.

River Rafting

River rafting is an adventure sport popular in Rishikesh and other rivers of Uttarakhand.
