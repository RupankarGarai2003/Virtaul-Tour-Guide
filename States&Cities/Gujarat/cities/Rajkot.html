Top Famous Tourist Places in Rajkot, Gujarat

Rajkot, known for its rich history and culture, is a vibrant city with several attractions that showcase its heritage and charm.

Mahatma Gandhi Museum

The Mahatma Gandhi Museum is located in the house where Gandhi spent his childhood. The museum houses a collection of photographs, documents, and personal items related to his life and teachings, making it a significant historical site.

Kaba Gandhi No Delo

Kaba Gandhi No Delo is the ancestral home of Mahatma Gandhi, where he lived during his childhood. The house has been converted into a museum that showcases various artifacts related to his early life, providing insight into the formative years of the leader.

Rajkot Railway Station

The Rajkot Railway Station is an architectural marvel and a major railway hub in Gujarat. It features a unique design and is surrounded by various shops and eateries, making it a lively place to visit.

Rotary Dolls Museum

The Rotary Dolls Museum is a unique attraction that features a vast collection of dolls from around the world. The museum aims to promote cultural awareness and understanding through the display of these dolls, representing various countries and cultures.

Aji Dam

Aji Dam is a beautiful spot located on the outskirts of Rajkot, offering serene views and a peaceful atmosphere. It's a popular picnic destination, where visitors can enjoy boating, nature walks, and breathtaking sunsets.

Nyari Dam

Nyari Dam is another picturesque spot near Rajkot, surrounded by lush greenery. The dam is a perfect getaway for nature lovers and offers opportunities for fishing and picnicking in a tranquil environment.
