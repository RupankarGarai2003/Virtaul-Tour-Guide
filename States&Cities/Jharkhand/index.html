Major Cities and Urban Centers

Ranchi

View on Google Maps

  • Dassam Falls: Majestic waterfall near Ranchi.
  • Hundru Falls: One of the highest waterfalls in Jharkhand.
  • Jonha Falls: Also known as Gautamdhara Falls.
  • Rock Garden: Beautiful garden with rock formations and sculptures.
  • Tagore Hill: A scenic spot with a view of Ranchi city.
  • Pahari Mandir: Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Ranchi Lake: Popular for boating and leisure activities.
  • Birsa Zoological Park: A zoo with a variety of wildlife species.

Jamshedpur

View on Google Maps

  • Jubilee Park: A well-maintained park with gardens and walking paths.
  • Tata Steel Zoological Park: Zoo with a range of animal species.
  • Dimna Lake: An artificial lake popular for picnics and boating.
  • Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary: Sanctuary known for its elephant population and diverse flora and fauna.
  • Russi Modi Centre of Excellence: A center showcasing Tata Steel's history and achievements.

Dhanbad

View on Google Maps

  • Maithon Dam: A multipurpose dam located on the Barakar River.
  • Panchet Dam: A dam located on the Damodar River.
  • Birsa Munda Park: A park dedicated to the tribal hero Birsa Munda.
  • Topchanchi Lake: A lake surrounded by scenic beauty.

Bokaro

View on Google Maps

  • Bokaro Steel Plant: Major steel plant in Bokaro.
  • Bokaro Steel City Park: A park with recreational facilities.
  • City Park: A popular park in the heart of Bokaro.
  • Jawaharlal Nehru Biological Park: A park with a variety of flora and fauna.
