Kashmir Valley

Srinagar

View on Google Maps

  • Dal Lake: Iconic lake with houseboats and shikara rides
  • Shalimar Bagh and Nishat Bagh: Mughal gardens with terraced lawns and fountains
  • Hazratbal Shrine: Muslim shrine with a pristine white marble mosque
  • Pari Mahal: Seven terraced garden overlooking Dal Lake

Gulmarg

View on Google Maps

  • Gulmarg Gondola: Cable car offering panoramic views of the Himalayas
  • Gulmarg Golf Course: Highest golf course in the world

Pahalgam

View on Google Maps

  • Betaab Valley: Scenic valley named after a Bollywood movie
  • Aru Valley: Picturesque valley with lush meadows and trekking trails
  • Lidder River: River offering opportunities for fishing and rafting

Jammu Region

Jammu City

View on Google Maps

  • Vaishno Devi Temple: Hindu pilgrimage site nestled in the Trikuta Mountains
  • Raghunath Temple: Largest temple complex in northern India
  • Mubarak Mandi Palace: Historic palace complex with architectural blend

Patnitop

View on Google Maps

  • Nathatop: Hill station offering panoramic views of snow-covered peaks
  • Sanasar Lake: Lake surrounded by lush green meadows, ideal for picnics

Ladakh Region

Leh

View on Google Maps

  • Leh Palace: Former royal palace offering views of Leh town and Stok Kangri range
  • Shanti Stupa: Peace Pagoda with panoramic views of Leh and surroundings
  • Thiksey Monastery and Hemis Monastery: Famous monasteries with ancient relics and festivals

Nubra Valley

View on Google Maps

  • Diskit Monastery: Oldest and largest Buddhist monastery in Nubra Valley
  • Hunder Sand Dunes: Desert landscape with double-humped Bactrian camels

Adventure Activities

  • Trekking and Mountaineering: Popular treks include Kashmir Great Lakes, Tarsar Marsar, and Markha Valley
  • River Rafting: White-water rafting in Lidder River (Pahalgam) and Zanskar River (Ladakh)

Festivals & Cultural Events

  • Ladakh Festival: Celebrated with traditional dance, music, and archery competitions
  • Tulip Festival (Srinagar): Held in April showcasing colorful tulip gardens
