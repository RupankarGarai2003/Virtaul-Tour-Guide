Major Cities and Urban Centers

Raipur

Mahant Ghasidas Memorial Museum, Vivekanand Sarovar (Burha Talab), Gandhi Udyan Park, Nandan Van Zoo and Safari.

Bilaspur

Achanakmar Wildlife Sanctuary, Ratanpur Temple, Malhar Archaeological Museum, Khutaghat Dam.

Durg-Bhilai

Maitri Bagh Zoo, Tandula Dam, Bhilai Steel Plant.

Jagdalpur

Chitrakote Falls, Tirathgarh Falls, Bastar Palace, Anthropological Museum.

Korba

Chaiturgarh (Lafagarh) Fort, Kendai Waterfall, Kosagaigarh.

Waterfalls and Natural Attractions

Chitrakote Falls

Known as the "Niagara of India", located on the Indravati River.

Tirathgarh Falls

Stunning waterfall in Kanger Valley National Park.

Amrit Dhara Waterfall

Beautiful waterfall near Koriya.

Kanger Valley National Park

Famous for its biodiversity, Kotumsar Cave, and Tirathgarh Falls.

Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary

Rich in flora and fauna, located in Mahasamund district.

Bhoramdeo Temple

Known as the "Khajuraho of Chhattisgarh", located near Kawardha.

Historical and Archaeological Sites

Sirpur

Archaeological site with ancient temples and Buddhist monasteries.

Ratanpur

Historical town with ancient temples and fort ruins.

Malhar

Archaeological site with ruins of ancient temples.

Barsoor

Known for ancient temples and archaeological significance.

Pilgrimage Sites

Danteshwari Temple

One of the 52 Shakti Peethas, located in Dantewada.

Rajiv Lochan Temple

Famous Vishnu temple in Rajim.

Champaran

Birthplace of the saint Vallabhacharya.

Bambleshwari Temple

Located on a hilltop in Dongargarh.

Cultural and Tribal Attractions

Bastar Dussehra

Unique 75-day-long festival celebrated in Jagdalpur.

Bastar Palace

Historical palace with rich cultural heritage.

Tribal Culture

Explore the tribal markets and handicrafts in Bastar and other regions.

Wildlife Sanctuaries and National Parks

Indravati National Park

Known for its population of wild buffaloes and other wildlife.

Sitanadi Wildlife Sanctuary

Known for its diverse flora and fauna, located in Dhamtari district.

Achanakmar Wildlife Sanctuary

Rich in biodiversity and part of Achanakmar-Amarkantak Biosphere Reserve.
